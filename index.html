Homework, Forum, Office Hours

Homework is due at the time specified, and can be submitted as an email attachment to buckingham-apcs2014@googlegroups.com. Content or homework-specific questions can be asked on the class forum. A teacher or TA will likely be able to respond to questions within 48 hours. Office hours are conducted on an as-needed basis. To request office hours, please send an email to buckingham-apcs2014@googlegroups.com, and we will work with you to set up a convenient time.
