Forum and Office Hours

Content-specific questions and be asked on the class forum. A teacher or TA will likely be able to respond to questions within 48 hours. Office hours are conducted on an as-needed basis. To request office hours, please send an email to buckingham-apcs2014@googlegroups.com, and we will work with you to set up a convenient time.

Homework

Assignments are due on Monday by midnight (unless otherwise specified) and can be submitted here. To submit an assignment, please name a file in the submission as follows: Lastname-Week#. The week number should be the week that the assingment was assigned, not the week that it is due. Refer to the week numbers below.
